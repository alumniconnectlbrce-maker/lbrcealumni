Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Current Year

Welcome to the LBRCE Alumni Connect Portal, a prestigious platform developed by Xeta Tech Solutions. We take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data Collection

We collect information primarily provided by the LBRCE administration (Roll Numbers, Names, Departments) to automate onboarding. Additional data like resumes, social links, and contact details are collected voluntarily when you build your profile.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used exclusively to facilitate institutional networking, mentorship matching, and job opportunity notifications. Xeta Tech Solutions and LBRCE do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security protocols, including role-based access control (RBAC) and encrypted databases, to ensure your academic and professional data remains strictly within the LBRCE ecosystem.

4. Communication Control

The platform uses a request-based messaging system. You retain full control over who can message you by accepting or rejecting connection requests.

Terms of Use

Agreement to Terms

By accessing the LBRCE Alumni Connect Portal, operated by LBRCE and developed by Xeta Tech Solutions, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Use. If you disagree with any part of the terms, you may not access the portal.

1. Eligibility & Access

This platform is exclusively restricted to verified students, alumni, coordinators, and administrators of Lakireddy Bali Reddy College of Engineering. Unauthorized access attempts are logged and strictly prohibited.

2. User Conduct

Users must maintain a professional demeanor. Harassment, spamming, posting fraudulent job opportunities, or sharing sensitive institutional data externally will result in immediate account suspension and potential administrative action.

3. Content Accuracy

Alumni posting job or internship opportunities are expected to provide accurate and truthful information. Students are expected to represent their skills and qualifications honestly in their resumes and portfolios.

4. Intellectual Property

The architecture, branding, code, and structural design of the platform are the intellectual property of Xeta Tech Solutions. The data within the platform remains the property of LBRCE and its respective users.

Core Functionality

Powerful Features for a Connected Campus

Designed specifically to maximize participation, simplify management, and provide real career value to our students.

Automated Onboarding

Admins create accounts using verified institutional data. Credentials are auto-generated and emailed, ensuring 100% user inclusion seamlessly.

Job & Internship Board

Alumni can post opportunities directly. Students search, filter, and apply seamlessly, creating a direct bridge to industry professionals.

Controlled Communication

Request-based messaging ensures professional interaction. Communication is enabled only after an alumni accepts a connection request.

Resume & Portfolios

Students build detailed profiles with resumes, skills, and projects. Alumni evaluate these profiles before offering referrals or mentorship.

Smart Notifications

Real-time, personalized notifications tailored by user role for job postings, events, and announcements ensuring updates are never missed.

Events & Mock Interviews

Departments organize virtual events, alumni meets, and mock interviews directly within the platform, facilitating real-time preparation.

Plus: Analytics & Donations

Comprehensive analytics dashboards for tracking user engagement and job trends. Secure and transparent donation handling for alumni to give back to institutional initiatives.
