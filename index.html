The Motivation

Moving from unstructured chats to a unified institutional ecosystem.

The Old Way (Problem)

Alumni interaction was unstructured and inconsistent. Data was scattered, communication informal, and valuable opportunities like mentorships, internships, and job referrals were often missed.

The New Portal (Solution)

A role-based, cloud-enabled platform connecting students, alumni, and admins in a controlled environment. Features automated onboarding, structured job postings, and controlled messaging.

Core Functionality

Powerful Features for a Connected Campus

Designed to maximize participation, simplify management, and provide real career value to our students.

Automated Onboarding

Admins create accounts using verified institutional data. Credentials are auto-generated and emailed, ensuring 100% user inclusion without manual registration dependency.

Job & Internship Board

Alumni can post opportunities directly. Students can search, filter, and apply seamlessly, creating a direct bridge to industry professionals and improving placements.

Controlled Communication

Request-based messaging ensures professional interaction. Communication is enabled only after an alumni accepts a student's connection request, preventing spam.

Resume & Portfolios

Students build detailed profiles with resumes, skills, and projects. Alumni can evaluate these profiles before offering referrals or valuable mentorship.

Smart Notifications

Real-time, personalized notifications tailored by user role and activity for job postings, events, and announcements ensuring relevant updates are never missed.

Events & Mock Interviews

Departments can organize virtual events, alumni meets, and mock interviews within the platform, facilitating real-time prep and networking.

Plus: Analytics & Donations

Comprehensive analytics dashboards for tracking user engagement and job trends. Secure and transparent donation handling for alumni to give back to institutional initiatives.

Access Control

Role-Based Experiences

Each user role has defined permissions, ensuring secure access, proper governance, and a structured workflow tailored to their specific needs.

Student

  • Complete Profile & Portfolio
  • Browse Jobs & Internships
  • Send Connection Requests
  • Apply for Jobs / Get Referrals
  • Register for Alumni Membership

Alumni

  • Post Jobs & Internships
  • View Student Profiles
  • Accept/Reject Requests
  • Provide Mentorship
  • Make Secure Donations

Coordinator

  • Organize Virtual Events
  • Schedule Mock Interviews
  • Monitor Participation
  • Manage Dept. Activities

Admin

  • Manage Users & Roles
  • Monitor System Activity
  • View Analytics Dashboard
  • Manage Notifications
  • Oversee Security

The Impact

Transforming Institutional Engagement

A sustainable, scalable ecosystem built to deliver measurable results for the college, students, and alumni.

Increased Alumni Engagement

Ensures 100% onboarding via automated creation. Converts passive alumni into active contributors, mentors, recruiters, and donors through structured, continuous interaction.

Improved Career Opportunities

Gives students direct access to verified jobs. Dramatically increases chances of referrals through network building, and enhances prep via mock interviews.

Centralized Alumni Database

Creates a single, structured platform for storing alumni data, eliminating scattered and outdated records. Enables highly efficient searching and filtering.

Data-Driven Decision Making

Analytics dashboard provides deep insights on user engagement, job postings, and participation trends to help the administration make informed strategic decisions.

Enhanced Institutional Branding

Showcases a strong, active network. Improves reputation among prospective students and recruiters, directly supporting accreditation and ranking requirements.

Sustainable & Scalable

Built on robust cloud infrastructure (AWS). Fully capable of handling increasing data securely, and ready for future enhancements like AI-based recommendations.
